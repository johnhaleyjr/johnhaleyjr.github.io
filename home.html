A weather app that doesn't display the weather?!

Ask yourself, when you are checking the weather, what I you trying to gain? You may think you want to know the temperature or the chance of rain, but you'd be wrong. What you really want to know, is if its going to be too cold and need to bring a jacket, or if you should cancel your plans to go rock climbing. So what you're really looking for is to know if you need to do those things based on some criteria and not really some set of numbers. Besides, we're all busy (and lazy), and don't have time to check the weather on our phones, for things we want to do, 5 days in advance.

Let this app do all that for you. Simply enter the event, location, date, and possibly the condition you want to be notified about (by default anytime the chance of rain is greater than 75%), and the app will notify you if its going to rain or if its too cold.


Don't allow yourself to check the weather every few hours to see if you need to change plans. The Lazy Weather app will periodically check the weather forecast for you, and if it looks like you need to change plans, it will notify you. Otherwise, the app won't bug you and you can assume your plans are fine.
